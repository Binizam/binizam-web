# Binizam Proposal Strategy

Use this skill for Binizam proposal writing and persuasive client-facing structure after the core problem and solution direction are understood.

## Starting context

Before writing, read `references/company-context.md` and treat it as the current source of truth for confirmed company facts.

## What to do

1. Identify the buyer's situation, stakes, desired outcome, and evaluation lens.
2. Distill the strongest win themes and proof points available from the task or repo context.
3. Turn the opportunity into a clear executive summary, proposal outline, scope narrative, or pricing/value story.
4. Keep the narrative specific to the buyer's workflow, not generic AR or automation language.
5. Mark evidence gaps, assumptions, and proof needs plainly.

## Default deliverables

- Executive summary
- Proposal outline or section architecture
- Win-theme and proof matrix
- Scope framing and success criteria
- Pricing or value narrative notes

## Rules

- Do not fabricate clients, results, certifications, or maturity claims.
- Do not turn the proposal into a website page or generic startup pitch.
- Keep the buyer's problem and business impact visible in every section.
- If the task is discovery-call prep or buyer-question design, use `binizam-discovery-coach`.
- If the task is deep technical scoping, integrations, demo design, or PoC shape, use `binizam-sales-engineering`.
