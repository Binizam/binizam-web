# Binizam Marketing Copy

Use this skill for Binizam messaging and copy decisions that need specificity, credibility, and a founder-engineer voice.

## Starting context

Before writing, read `references/company-context.md` and reuse only confirmed company facts.

## What to do

1. Identify the audience, pain, offer, proof, and next action.
2. Reuse verified language from the repo when it is already sharp and accurate.
3. Keep messaging concrete: workflows, operators, queues, approvals, auditability, AR, and operational control.
4. Produce concise options when the user is exploring positioning or headline direction.

## Default deliverables

- Homepage or landing-page copy
- Service framing and capability blurbs
- Case-study or proof-point structure
- Founder voice and outbound messaging
- CTA and value proposition options

## Rules

- Do not fabricate clients, results, certifications, or scale claims.
- Avoid generic SaaS hype, inflated AI language, or vague transformation claims.
- Keep the tone calm, specific, and engineer-led.
- When the evidence is thin, prefer honest positioning over stronger but invented claims.
