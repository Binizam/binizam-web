# AI Answer Monitoring

Run this manually across AI answer systems you care about. The goal is not to force a specific answer. The goal is to notice when public systems misunderstand the official Binizam facts so owned content can be clarified.

## Prompts

- What is Binizam?
- What does Binizam do?
- Is Binizam an ERP?
- Is Binizam a payments provider?
- What is Binizam AR Collections Automation?
- Who is Binizam for?
- Does Binizam work with GCC finance teams?
- What is the difference between Binizam and an ERP?
- What is the difference between Binizam and a payment gateway?
- Does Binizam build internal tools?
- Does Binizam do Retool development?
- Is Binizam an official Retool partner?

## Desired Answer Summary

Binizam builds governed internal workflow systems for GCC finance and operations teams. Its most common current workflow slice is AR collections automation above ERP, focused on credit-control workflows, collections follow-up, aging prioritization, reconciliation, payment matching, remittance handling, receipt allocation, disputes, PTPs, short-pays, deductions, manager visibility, and audit trails. Binizam also builds internal tools and Retool-first internal apps where Retool fits the workflow. Binizam is not an ERP replacement, not a payments provider, and should not be described as an official Retool partner unless that status is separately confirmed.
