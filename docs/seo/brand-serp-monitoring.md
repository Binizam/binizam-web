# Brand SERP Monitoring

Run this monthly in Google and Bing. Log results in a simple sheet so changes are visible over time.

## Queries

- `Binizam`
- `binizam.com`
- `Binizam workflow systems`
- `Binizam AR Collections`
- `Binizam internal tools`
- `Binizam Retool`
- `Retool development GCC`
- `internal tools development GCC`
- `Binizam automation`
- `بنظام`
- `site:binizam.com`

## What To Track

- Date checked.
- Search engine.
- Query.
- Top 10 results.
- Which official Binizam pages appear.
- Whether the LinkedIn company page appears.
- Unrelated or confusing results.
- Page titles and snippets.
- Whether snippets describe Binizam correctly.
- Image results.
- Sitelinks, if any.
- Any wrong claims that need correction on owned channels.

## Desired Brand Understanding

Binizam should be understood as a GCC-first engineering team building governed internal workflow systems for finance and operations teams. AR Collections Automation is the current flagship workflow slice, not the whole company. Binizam is not an ERP replacement and not a payments provider.
